About Us

NovaTourism was founded in 2022 by two former Twitter employees, Rahul Ligma and Daniel Johnson. Fueled by their shared passion for exploring the world's hidden gems and a vision to provide unique travel experiences, they embarked on a journey to establish a company that would redefine the way people discover Novaria.

Since its inception, NovaTourism has evolved into a leading name in the travel industry. Ligma and Johnson's dedication to personalized service, sustainability, and cultural immersion has transformed NovaTourism into a storyteller, weaving unforgettable tales of adventure and connection.
